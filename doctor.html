Dr Edwin Guan

Founder and Medical Director

With over 16 years of experience in the field of Aesthetic Medicine, Dr
Guan leads a team of professional and dedicated aesthetic consultants and
therapists here in La Ville esthétique.

Dr Guan specialises in combating anti-aging for the face, skin and body. His mission is to revitalise the natural radiance in his patients, renewing confidence and balance so that they feel beautiful in their own skin.

Driven by a deep passion to hone his craft, Dr Guan is constantly refining carefully selected techniques and finding new ways to make medical aesthetics better, safer and more comfortable for his patients.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (Singapore)
  • MSc Practical Dermatology (Cardiff)

Membership in Professional Organisations

  • American Academy of Anti-Aging Medicine (Board Certified)
  • Society of Aesthetic Medicine (Singapore)
