The Secret To That Youthful Glow

Why Rest, Eye Creams and Facials Are Not Enough

A person’s facial wrinkles are a giveaway of their age. Even laugh lines – a reflection of joyous living – are seen as woeful wrinkles or crows feet that need to be banished. So, how can we restore our youthful complexion?

What causes facial wrinkles?

Before we get to the solution, we have to dig into the cause of the problem. What are the causes for tired and aging skin? Some of the leading causes include UV exposure, skin dryness, and the loss of collagen and elasticity that come with age.

The hard truth? No amount of facials, creams and cold presses can reverse these physiological effects and help you escape the passage of time.

Restoring freshness to Wrinkles and aging skin

Thankfully, not all is lost! Aesthetic treatments such as facial thread lift and voluma fillers can significantly improve your skin’s condition. These are safe, minimally-invasive treatments that are proven to target signs of aging and give you a youthful glow. Such treatments create a natural, yet noticable improvement to your skin.

Take a look at these popular treatments to tackle signs of aging:

Facial Thread Lift

With instant results and low downtime, Facial Thread Lift is an instant favourite at La Ville Esthétique.

Thread lift helps to tighten your skin by inserting medical threads into your face, then “pulling” your skin by tightening the thread.
