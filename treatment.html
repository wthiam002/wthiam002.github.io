What is Tread Lifting?

A cosmetic procedure that offers a minimally invasive alternative to facelift surgery.

Thread lift helps to tighten your skin by inserting medical threads into your face, then
“pulling” your skin by tightening the thread.

Thread Procedures

Step 1:

Anaesthesia

Before the procedure, our doctor will prescribe anaesthesia to reduce pain and downtime for treatment areas.

Step 2:

Incision

A thin cannula will be used to insert the threads underneath your skin.

Step 3:

Tightening

You may feel light pressure or tightening under your skin. Once tightened, the tread ends will be cut, and no stitches are required

Step 4:

Results

Within a few minutes of the cannula being taken out, your procedure will be completed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Thread lifts are performed after administering local anaesthesia. At LVE, we aim to provide painless
treatment to ensure you have the best experience with us.

The number of threads you will require will mostly rely on the area that you want to treat and the degree of skin laxity you have.

Patients take one to two weeks to completely recover from a thread lift procedure. At LVE, we are committed to your well-being. We provide aftercare services for our patients to maximise the benefits of their treatments and help them reach their beauty goals faster.

Thread lifts are low risk. After a thread lift, there is almost no risk of scarring, suffering from significant bruising, bleeding, or experiencing other complications.
